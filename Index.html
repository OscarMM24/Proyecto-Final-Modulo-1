Wu-Tang Clan


The Wu-Tang Clan is a hip-hop group from Staten Island, New York that formed in 1992. The group's name comes from the Taoist holy mountain Wu Dang in China, and was inspired by the kung fu film Shaolin and Wu Tang. The Wu-Tang Clan is considered to be one of the most important groups in the history of East Coast hip hop.



Nasir jones

Nas (born September 14, 1973, Brooklyn, New York, U.S.) is an American rapper and songwriter who became a dominant voice in 1990s East Coast hip-hop. Nas built a reputation as an expressive chronicler of inner-city street life.
